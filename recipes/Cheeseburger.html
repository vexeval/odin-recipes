Cheese burger

Description

A simplistic but tasty Cheese burger. Enjoy making this pizza super fast in about 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup fat-free milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add milk and oil and mix well.
  3. Form a dough into a ball and place in a greased 9-inch square baking pan.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
